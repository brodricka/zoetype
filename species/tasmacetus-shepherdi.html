Shadow
Shepherd's beaked whale is so rarely encountered that its behavior remains scientifically unknown after decades of study. Your inaccessibility is genuine and it costs you something real. The people who want to know you hit the same wall that marine biologists hit, not because you are withholding but because the depth is simply where you live. What it costs you is the specific intimacy that only becomes possible when someone can follow you all the way down, and almost no one can.

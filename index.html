What is Zoëtype

A personality framework rooted in the natural world.

Most personality tests sort you into one of a dozen predetermined types. A leader. An empath. An introvert. These categories exist because they are easy to build and easy to market. They are not particularly true.

Zoëtype works from a different premise. Every animal on earth evolved a specific way of surviving, relating, processing, and moving through the world. That specificity is real data. Zoëtype uses it to find the species whose behavioral profile most closely matches yours across 25 measurable dimensions.

The result is one specific animal out of more than a thousand.

Built from real science
Every species is drawn from the Global Biodiversity Information Facility, a database of peer-reviewed biological records. Each one is scored across 25 behavioral dimensions using documented research on how that species actually lives, hunts, bonds, and survives. The match is calculated from that data.
Specific by design
Other tests pick 12 to 50 familiar animals and build categories around them. Every person who takes those tests lands in one of the same handful of results. Zoëtype draws from 1,168 species and growing. The specificity is the entire point. A result that applies to millions of people tells you nothing about yourself.
A system, not a quiz
Most personality quizzes give you a label and move on. Zoëtype gives you a lens. Understanding your animal opens into questions about how you love, how you work, what drains you, who you are compatible with, and what your shadow is costing you. The animal is where it starts, not where it ends.
How it works

From 96 questions to one specific animal.

01
You take the test
96 questions covering the full range of who you are. The test takes 30 to 45 minutes and is free to complete.
02
Your profile is scored
Your answers generate a score across 25 dimensions, including things like cognitive style, attachment pattern, relationship to risk, social architecture, and shadow traits.
03
The match is found
Your dimensional profile is compared against 1,168 species, each scored on the same 25 axes. The closest match across all dimensions becomes your Zoëtype.
04
You receive your result
A full portrait of your animal and what it reveals about you. Personality, shadow, ecological role, species facts, and the option to go deeper with the full Zoëtype Report.
